Sponsors FAQ

  • You will get access to the resumes of participants
  • You will be allowed access to the event
  • You will be allowed to talk with participants during the event
  • You will be allowed to see the presentations of the challenge winners

Challenge Requirements

  1. Sponsors will need to provide a subject expert to advise hackers on what the challenge means. For example, saying "machine learning rhinoceros'" and leaving would not be acceptable. Stay and explain, please.
  2. Sponsors will be given a designated area for their challenges. This way you will know who is working on your challenge. If a sponsor wishes to host multiple challenges, we will do everything we can to keep the challenges as close as possible.
  3. Sponsors will be responsible for judging their challenges. Who you send to judge is entirely up to you. Recommendations would be an engineer knowledgeable of the tech and topic and an HR representative.
  4. Sponsors are responsible for awarding their challenge winners.

Contact Us

If you are interested in collaborating with us to host a challenge at our event and willing to meet the requirements above, please use the form to contact us.
