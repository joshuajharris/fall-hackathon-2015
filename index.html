October 2nd through the 4th

This season, the Strome Entrepreneurial Center at ODU will host "Monarchs Hack the Campus"! Where hackers will have the opportunity to come and address multiple challenges. Each interesting, unique, judged, and awarded separately from the others.

#HappyHacking

Learn

The best way to learn is by doing! Experiment with new languages or platforms! Learn simply for the sake of knowing.

Innovate

Hack for change, hack to change, or just change the way we all hack! Innovate for the sake of innovation.

Community

The best thing about learning something new is to find the community that is driving it! We are the Monarch Community.

Challenges

Start developping your ideas now!

Monarch Challenge

This season, the Monach challenge, is to address a social issue. Some examples would be: a website to educate on cyber bullying, a mobile application to help support wounded veterans, or developing a platform for non-profits to conduct food drives.

NASA Develop Challenge

We are excited to be partnering with NASA Develop to provide hackers access to NASA GIS data! The door is completely open here, we are all excited to see what you can do with this data!
